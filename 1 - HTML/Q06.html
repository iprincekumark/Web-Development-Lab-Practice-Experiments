B.Tech Computer Science & Technology

Department introduction :

BTech Computer Science is a 4-year degree course offered to students at the undergraduate level. It aims
to provide students with intricate knowledge of computer technologies and functional operations along
with programming, coding, Web and database development.

  1. Full Form of the Course: Bachelors of Technology in Computer Science.
  2. Duration: 4 years
  3. Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics + Entrance Exam acceptable by the university.
  4. Higher Studies: M.Tech Computer Science, M.Tech Information Technology, MBA, PhD, etc.
  5. Job Options: Technical Support Manager, Software Engineer, Testing Engineer, System Analyst etc.
